I am motivated by a desire to comprehend the things I interact with. My dad likes to tell a story where apparently, I refused to use the toilet until he showed me how the plumbing worked and that flushing did not simply dump waste water under the house.

The moment I was introduced to the family desktop I wanted to know how people had tricked a bunch of metal and plastic to effectively think using electricity. Years later that same curiosity led me to intern with my middle school IT department, learn Linux for my science fair project, take AP-Java programming in high school and eventually attend Holberton School for software engineering. At Holberton I completed the full-stack web development course.
